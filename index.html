Natacha Larnaud is an international French student at the University of Miami majoring in journalism and minoring in sociology, motion pictures and Spanish. After having had the chance to grow up in several countries such as Spain, Argentina, Morocco and the United Arab Emirates, Natacha has the ability to speak 4 languages fluently. This multicultural life has given her the open-mindedness to connect and be familiar with different religions and ethnicities, and those are the factors that made her want to become a journalist. Her journlalistic interests include politics, societal issues, sports and entertainment.
